### What "Crisis" Actually Means by Type
- **Equity drawdown ≥35%** + duration ≥12 months = severe bear market (1929, 1973, 2000, 2007, 2020)
- **Banking collapse + credit freeze** = systemic crisis (1929, 2008, mini: 2023)
- **Currency crisis + EM contagion** = regional/EM (1982, 1994, 1997)
- **Burst of asset bubble** = valuation-driven (1990 Japan, 2000 dot-com)

**Critical distinction**: dot-com 2000 caused a **deep equity bear market and mild recession**, NOT a banking collapse. The current AI setup looks like that pattern.
