# 100 Years of Financial Crises: Predictive Indicators & the 2026 AI Boom
*Research summary — July 12, 2026*

## 1. Crises reviewed
1929 (Great Crash/Depression), 1937, 1969–70, 1973–74, 1987, 1990 S&L/Japan, 1997–98 Asia/LTCM, 2000 dot-com, 2007–09 GFC, 2020 COVID, 2022 bear market.

## 2. Indicators ranked by predictive strength

| Rank | Indicator | Hit rate / evidence | Lead time | Predicts |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Yield curve inversion (10Y–3M)** | Every US recession since 1969 (Estrella–Mishkin 1996/98; NY Fed model) | 6–24 mo, avg ~14 | Recession timing |
| 2 | **BIS credit-to-GDP gap > +10pp** | Best univariate banking-crisis predictor (Borio–Lowe 2002; Drehmann–Juselius); Basel III CCyB anchor | 1–3 yrs | Banking crises |
| 3 | **Shiller CAPE > 30** | Only 1929, 1999, 2007, 2021, now; predicts near-zero 10-yr real returns | Poor timing (yrs) | Crash magnitude, not date |
| 4 | **Margin debt/GDP + YoY growth > 50%** | Peaks months before 1929, 2000, 2007, 2021 tops | 0–12 mo | Market tops |
| 5 | **HY OAS < 350bp then widening** | Tights at ~250bp mid-2007; widening is the trigger | 3–12 mo | Credit events |
| 6 | Buffett indicator, concentration, real Fed funds, capex/revenue gap | Contextual (Kindleberger–Minsky cycle, Reinhart–Rogoff patterns) | — | Fragility |

**Key structural finding:** valuation indicators answer *IF* (crash-prone), credit/monetary indicators answer *WHEN*. No method has ever timed a crisis precisely; the best give 6–24 month windows. 1987- and 2020-type events (rate shock, exogenous) are largely unpredictable by any of these.

## 4. Verdict by this framework
Composite ~72/100 (elevated). **Bubble conditions ~95/100** — rivaling 1999/2021. **Timing triggers ~44/100** — the two most reliable century-long timing signals (curve, credit gap) have not fired. Setup most resembles **1998–99 or 2006**: late-boom with historically 1–3 years of runway, countdown starting if/when the curve inverts, HY spreads widen past ~400bp, or AI revenue disappoints. A 1987-style crash-without-recession remains possible anytime.
